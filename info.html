Fun Facts About Websites!

  • The very first website ever made is still online! You can visit it here.
  • Websites are made using special "languages" like HTML and CSS. They help build and decorate the page!
  • The person who made the first website is named Tim Berners-Lee. He’s like a real-life internet inventor!
  • Over a **billion** websites exist today. Many of them are just for fun or practice!
  • Every time you open a website, your computer turns code into pictures, words, and buttons. It’s like website magic!
